Introduction to RA2CE — Live Webinar
--------------------------------------

A series of webinars will be hosted to introduce RA2CE to the community, demonstrate its capabilities, and provide hands-on training. The first webinar was held on 26 March 2026 and covered the core analysis modules of RA2CE using real data and Jupyter Notebooks.
More webinars will be scheduled in the future, so stay tuned for announcements on our website and social media channels.
